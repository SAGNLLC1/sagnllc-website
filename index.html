About Us

Built to Serve. Ready to Deliver.

SAGN LLC is a Miami-Dade based, woman-owned small business founded by Joanne Seepersad. We specialize in providing dependable general services and supply chain solutions to federal, state, and local government agencies.

From janitorial and grounds maintenance to industrial procurement and vendor sourcing, SAGN LLC offers the agility of a small business with the professionalism that government contracts demand. Our owner holds a background in commercial facility services, procurement operations, and administrative management, bringing practical experience to every contract we pursue. We are SAM.gov registered, listed on MyFloridaMarketPlace, and prepared to accept purchase orders through standard government procurement channels.

As a new entrant in the government contracting space, we bring fresh energy, competitive pricing, and an owner-managed approach where accountability is never delegated. We welcome teaming arrangements and mentor-protégé partnerships with established primes.

Core Competencies

What We Do

Janitorial & Custodial Services

Comprehensive cleaning and sanitation services for government buildings, offices, and public facilities. We provide reliable scheduling, documented quality standards, and direct owner oversight on every engagement. Services include routine cleaning, deep sanitization, floor care, and restroom maintenance.

NAICS 561720

Landscaping & Grounds Maintenance

Professional landscape maintenance for government properties and public installations. Services include mowing, tree and shrub trimming, irrigation management, seasonal planting, and general grounds upkeep. Delivered through established subcontractor partnerships with licensed landscaping professionals in the South Florida region.

NAICS 561730

Industrial Equipment Sourcing

Procurement and delivery of industrial and commercial equipment, machinery, and heavy-duty tools through our network of vetted suppliers. We source competitively, verify TAA and Berry Amendment compliance where required, and handle logistics from quote through delivery.

NAICS 423840

Service & Cleaning Equipment Supply

End-to-end sourcing of janitorial equipment, cleaning machines, sanitation supplies, and facility maintenance products. We leverage supplier relationships to deliver competitive pricing on commercial-grade equipment for government facilities.

NAICS 423850

Office Supplies & Commodities

Procurement and delivery of office supplies, paper products, breakroom essentials, and general-use commodities. Fast turnaround on quotes with competitive pricing structures tailored to government purchasing requirements.

NAICS 424120

Vendor Sourcing & RFQ Response

Comprehensive vendor identification, qualification, and supply chain coordination across all our service lines. We respond quickly to Requests for Quotation with clear, compliant proposals and competitive pricing. Our established supplier network enables us to deliver accurate quotes and meet tight procurement deadlines.

Track Record

Building Our Track Record

SAGN LLC is a newly established government contractor actively building its contract performance record. While we are new to the government contracting space, our owner brings direct, hands-on experience from the private sector:

  • Facility Services: Practical experience in commercial cleaning operations, including scheduling, supply management, quality control, and client communication for multi-site cleaning engagements.
